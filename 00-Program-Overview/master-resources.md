## Professional Associations

### ISACA (isaca.org)

**Why join:** ISACA is the premier professional body for GRC professionals. They own the CISA, CRISC, CGEIT, and CISM certifications. Membership provides access to the ISACA Journal, research publications, webinars, local chapter events, and the ISACA online community.

- **Free:** ISACA Cybersecurity Fundamentals Certificate (entry-level; no experience required)
- **Membership cost:** ~£120/year
- **Certifications:** CISA (IS Audit), CRISC (Risk Management), CGEIT (Governance), CISM (Security Management)
- **Local chapters:** Find your nearest chapter at isaca.org/chapters

### IIA — Institute of Internal Auditors (theiia.org)

**Why join:** For audit-focused GRC roles. IIA owns the CIA (Certified Internal Auditor) certification and publishes the IPPF (International Professional Practices Framework) which defines internal audit standards.

### IAPP — International Association of Privacy Professionals (iapp.org)

**Why join:** For GDPR and privacy-focused GRC roles. IAPP owns the CIPP/E (Certified Information Privacy Professional/Europe) certification — increasingly required for DPO and privacy compliance roles.

### BCI — Business Continuity Institute (thebci.org)

**Why join:** For business continuity and resilience roles. BCI owns the CBCI (Certificate of the BCI) certification. Good Practice Guidelines are free and excellent.
