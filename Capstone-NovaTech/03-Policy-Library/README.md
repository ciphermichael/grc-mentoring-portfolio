## Policy Quality Standards

Each policy **must** include:
- Document header: Policy ID, Version, Owner, Approved By, Effective Date, Review Date, ISO ref, PCI-DSS ref, GDPR ref
- Purpose and Scope (applies to all 2,000 NovaTech employees and contractors)
- Policy Statements in "must/shall" language — no "should consider"
- Roles and Responsibilities
- Enforcement consequences
- Review history table
- **NovaTech-specific context** — not generic templates
