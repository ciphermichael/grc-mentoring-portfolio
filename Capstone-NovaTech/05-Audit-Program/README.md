## Audit Independence Requirement

> **Critical:** The Internal Audit function (co-sourced with KPMG) reports to the **Board Risk and Audit Committee**, NOT to the CISO. This independence is required by:
> - ISO 27001:2022 Clause 9.2
> - IIA IPPF Standards
> - ISO 19011:2018 — Audit Principles
