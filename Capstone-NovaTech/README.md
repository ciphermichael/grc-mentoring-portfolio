## 🏢 Client: NovaTech Corp

**Company:** NovaTech Corp  
**Industry:** UK Fintech — Payment Processing  
**Size:** 2,000 employees  
**Location:** London, UK  
**Customers:** 5 million retail payment customers  
**Revenue:** £180M ARR

### Business Context

NovaTech Corp is a rapidly growing UK fintech company processing consumer payments and holding personal data for 5 million customers. They are under increasing regulatory pressure from the FCA and are preparing for enterprise sales that require ISO 27001 certification.

**Current State:**
- No ISO 27001 or SOC 2 certification
- No formal security audit programme
- Informal vendor management
- Risk register exists but unmanaged
- GDPR DPO appointed but programme immature

**Your Mission:** Build a complete, enterprise-grade GRC programme from the ground up.

## 🎤 Board Presentation

In Week 24, you will present your complete GRC programme to a panel of mentors acting as the NovaTech Board of Directors.

**Format:** 30-minute presentation + 15-minute Q&A  
**Audience:** Board of Directors (non-technical)  
**Required:** Live GitHub portfolio demonstration

### Expected Questions
- "Walk me through your risk register — how did you decide what to prioritize?"
- "How long will it take to achieve ISO 27001 certification?"
- "What would a data breach cost us if we don't fix the vendor gap?"
- "Why should we invest £205k in the PAM and segmentation projects?"
- "How do you know this GRC programme is actually working?"
