# 📁 Example Evidence Files

> These are **completed sample deliverables** showing the expected quality, format, and depth of professional GRC work.

---

## Purpose

Before starting your assignments, **review these files**. They show:

- What professional GRC documents look like
- The level of detail expected
- How to format data in Excel and PowerPoint
- The tone and language used in real GRC reports

---

## 📊 XLS Files — Excel Evidence Samples

### `Sample-Risk-Register.xlsx`
**Simulates:** CloudSafe Ltd. Enterprise Risk Register  
**Scenario:** Week 2 assignment output  
**Contents:**
- **Risk Register tab:** 20 risks scored across 5 categories with RAG-rated inherent/residual scores, treatment plans, and owners
- **Risk Heatmap tab:** Colour-coded 5x5 risk matrix
- **Risk Summary tab:** Dashboard with risk metrics and treatment breakdown

**Key learning points:**
- How to structure a risk register for a real organization
- How to colour-code risk ratings (Critical = Red, High = Orange, Medium = Yellow, Low = Green)
- How to write clear, specific risk descriptions

---

### `Sample-ISO27001-Gap-Assessment.xlsx`
**Simulates:** Xown Solutions Limited ISO 27001:2022 SoA + Gap Assessment  
**Scenario:** Week 5 assignment output  
**Contents:**
- **SoA – Gap Assessment tab:** All 93 Annex A controls assessed with maturity scoring (0–5), gap identification, justification, owner, priority, and status
- **Domain Summary tab:** Aggregated view by domain (Organizational, People, Physical, Technological)

**Key learning points:**
- How a Statement of Applicability (SoA) is structured
- How to use a 0–5 maturity scale for ISO 27001 controls
- How to identify and document gaps professionally

---

### `Sample-Control-Testing-Workbook.xlsx`
**Simulates:** FinancePro Ltd. Internal Audit — Control Testing  
**Scenario:** Week 12 assignment output  
**Contents:**
- **Control Testing Workbook tab:** 15 controls tested with detailed test procedures, evidence collected, test results (PASS/PARTIAL/FAIL), and deficiency documentation
- **Deficiency Register tab:** 12 findings classified by severity (Critical/High/Medium/Low) with root causes, risk impact, and remediation recommendations

**Key learning points:**
- How to write audit test procedures
- How to document test results and evidence
- How to classify and write up control deficiencies

---

### `Sample-Vendor-Risk-Register.xlsx`
**Simulates:** DataBridge Corp Vendor Risk Management  
**Scenario:** Week 8 assignment output  
**Contents:**
- **Vendor Risk Register tab:** 10 vendors assessed with tier classification, certification evidence, contractual clauses, monitoring frequency, and risk rating
- **VRAQ – Sample Questions tab:** 15 sample VRAQ questions across 8 domains

**Key learning points:**
- How to tier-classify vendors (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- What fields a professional vendor risk register includes
- How to structure a Vendor Risk Assessment Questionnaire (VRAQ)

## 📊 PPT Files — PowerPoint Evidence Samples

### `Sample-Executive-Risk-Report.pptx`
**Simulates:** TechCorp Quarterly Board Risk Report  
**Scenario:** Week 15 assignment output  
**Slides:**
1. Title slide (professional branded cover)
2. Executive Summary (overall RAG status + domain breakdown)
3. Risk Dashboard (KPIs, Top 5 risks table)
4. Compliance Status (framework progress bars)
5. Security KPI Dashboard (10 KPIs with RAG and trends)
6. Security Incidents (Q1 summary + notable incidents)
7. Vendor Risk Status (vendor scorecard)
8. Risk Trend Analysis (bar chart + observations)
9. Board Recommendations (5 prioritized actions)
10. Next Steps (Q2 roadmap)

**Key learning points:**
- How to communicate risk to non-technical executives
- How to use RAG status effectively
- How to build a compelling board presentation

---

### `Sample-Board-Security-Briefing.pptx`
**Simulates:** NovaTech Corp Quarterly Board Security Briefing  
**Scenario:** Week 24 capstone presentation  
**Slides:**
1. Professional cover slide
2. CISO Overview (3-minute summary + 3 things board should know)
