## Purpose

This gap assessment evaluates the organisation's current information security management practices against the requirements of ISO/IEC 27001:2022, including the mandatory Clauses (4–10) and all 93 Annex A controls. Results are used to build the ISO 27001 implementation roadmap and Statement of Applicability (SoA).

---

## Maturity Scoring Scale

| Score | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **0** | Not Existent | No controls or processes in place. Complete absence. |
| **1** | Initial / Ad Hoc | Some controls exist informally; undocumented; applied inconsistently. |
| **2** | Developing | Controls documented but not consistently applied; partial implementation. |
| **3** | Defined | Controls fully documented; consistently applied; staff aware. |
| **4** | Managed | Controls measured and monitored; metrics tracked; management visibility. |
| **5** | Optimised | Controls continuously improved; lessons learned applied; best-in-class. |

**Target for ISO 27001 certification: Level 3 minimum across all applicable controls**

---

## Gap Priority Definitions

| Priority | Description | Certification Impact |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Critical** | Major non-conformance — will fail Stage 2 audit | Must remediate before Stage 2 |
| **High** | Minor non-conformance or significant gap | Must remediate before Stage 2 |
| **Medium** | Improvement needed — not a non-conformance | Remediate within 6 months |
| **Low** | Best practice improvement — not required for certification | Remediate in Year 2 |
